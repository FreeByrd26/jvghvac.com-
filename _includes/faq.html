Tell me about California's 2027 furnace phase-out.

California is phasing out new natural-gas furnace installations by 2027 to meet clean-air goals. Heat pumps heat AND cool your home with electricity, so they're the future of California comfort. We've installed them for years and can help you plan the transition before the deadline.
