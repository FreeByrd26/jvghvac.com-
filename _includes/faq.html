What are the new regulations for gas furnaces and water heaters in California?

California is moving away from gas-burning home appliances. Under the state's zero-emission appliance standards, new residential gas furnaces and gas water heaters are being phased out beginning in 2027, with electric heat pump models becoming the standard. Heat pump HVAC systems and heat pump water heaters meet the new rules — we install both and can help you plan the switch before the deadline so you're never caught off guard.
